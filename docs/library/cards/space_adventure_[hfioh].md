**Description**:

Space Adventure brings together some incredible audio recordings from NASA of some of the pioneering events in space exploration. Inspire your children to look to the stars as we hear Neil Armstrong taking his "one small step for man, one giant leap for mankind" and listen to the compelling original audio of "Houston, We have a problem..." that inspired the Apollo 13 movie starring Tom Hanks. Children's imagination is fired with atmospheric rocket launches and astronauts describing the Russian Space Station Mir.
